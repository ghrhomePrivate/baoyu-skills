# Notion 风格

极简手绘线稿、知性气质。

## 元素组合

```yaml
canvas:
  ratio: portrait-3-4
  grid: single | dual

image_effects:
  cutout: clean
  stroke: none | white-solid
  filter: none | muted-tones

typography:
  decorated: none | handwritten
  tags: black-white | pill
  direction: horizontal

decorations:
  emphasis: circle-mark | underline
  background: solid-white | paper-texture
  doodles: hand-drawn-lines | arrows-curvy
  frames: none | rounded-rect
```

## 色板

| 角色 | 颜色 | Hex |
|------|--------|-----|
| Primary | 黑、深灰 | #1A1A1A, #4A4A4A |
| Background | 纯白、米白 | #FFFFFF, #FAFAFA |
| Accents | 粉彩蓝、粉彩黄、粉彩粉 | #A8D4F0, #F9E79F, #FADBD8 |

## 视觉元素

- 简单线条涂鸦、手绘轻微抖动
- 几何形、火柴人式简笔画
- 大量留白、单线粗细墨水感
- 干净、不拥挤的版式

## 字体

- 清爽手绘字母/文字
- 简单无衬线标签
- 文字装饰极少
